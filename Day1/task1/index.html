Quick Hummus Recipe

Quick, tasty hummus — adapted from a number of recipes. Great with salad, grilled meats, and pitta bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) chick peas (garbanzo beans)
  • 175 g tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • ½ red pepper
  • A pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • A dash olive oil

Instructions

  1. Remove the skin from the garlic, and chop coarsely.
  2. Remove seeds and stalk from the pepper, and chop coarsely.
  3. Add all ingredients into a food processor.
  4. Process until you reach the desired texture:
    • Short processing — coarse / chunky hummus.
    • Longer processing — smooth hummus.

Variations

Try adding lemon and coriander, chili pepper, lime and chipotle, harissa and mint, or spinach and feta. Experiment to find your favourite!

Storage

Refrigerate in a sealed container. Use within one week. If it becomes fizzy, throw it away.

Hummus can be frozen — thaw and use within a couple of months.
